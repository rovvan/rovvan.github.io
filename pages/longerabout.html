About

Rowan Lynch is a curator and artist from Hamilton, ON, based in Toronto. They are the recipient of OCAD University's 2018 medal in Criticism and Curatorial Studies and have completed independant projects with OCADU galleries, the Hamilton Audio Visual Node (HAVN), Xpace Cultural Centre, and Art Metropole. They are currently a curatorial assistant with Critical Distance Centre for Curators.
