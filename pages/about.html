ABOUT

Founded in 2019 as an artist-run space, Hearth seeks to provide a site to present projects within a context that values collaboration, experimentation, and community. Until November 2021 we were located on Ulster St. just east of Three Star Variety (621 Bathurst, Toronto, ON, M5S 2R2).

As a structural element in the makeup of a house, and a tool providing warmth, light, and food; a hearth gathers us towards itself, and towards each other.

All programming is collaboratively produced by

Health and Safety Guidelines

Hearth aims to provide a reprieve from limitations imposed by many larger artistic operating bodies, and the city itself.

Hearth is committed to working towards an anti-oppressive, queer positive environment, and welcoming marginalized and racialized folks through programming that celebrates the work of a diverse range of emerging collaborators.
